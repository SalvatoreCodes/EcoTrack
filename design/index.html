EcoRoute to SCBD avoids the Jl. Gatot Subroto hotspot where air quality is poor right now and traffic is idling, which is where most CO₂ gets burned. It's +5 min but −38% emissions (saves 1.2 kg).
Is biking actually realistic today?
For 8.6 km, yes: it's flat, ~31 min, and air along the canal path is in the good range (AQI 41). Only catch is rain forecast after 4 PM. If you leave before then, bike is the greenest and you skip the parking.
What about TransJakarta?
Corridor 1 runs Kemang→Sudirman every ~6 min. ~28 min door-to-door, 0.4 kg CO₂ (shared). Best mix of low-emission and low-effort if you'd rather not pedal.
